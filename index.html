Mother's Day · 2026

To the Woman
Who Holds Us Together

A letter from the heart

Dear Mama,

There are no words grand enough to describe everything you mean to me, but every year I try, because you deserve to hear it. Even after all those hardships, kaguul, kasakit na ato naagian, you still chose to be strong — yes, you chose the role of being the strongest person sa ato pamilya.

Those quiet sacrifices that went unnoticed until I was old enough to notice them all at once, and feel the full weight of how much you gave.

You taught me how to be strong. You taught me to express my emotions as they are not meant to be bottled up. You taught me to appreciate things I once took for granted. I carry all of that with me everywhere I go.

Today, I want you to be celebrated. To be recognized. To be acknowledged. And to be noticed. I want you to know that everything you ever planted in me is still growing — and I am so grateful it was you who did the planting.

With all the love I have, Dabs ♡
